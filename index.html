South Forest Center

FUN FOR ALL

Introducing South Forest Center: an exciting, new Family Entertainment Center in Worcester, Massachussetts. Come one, come all, to see our beuatiful Ferris Wheel, vintage Drive-In Movie Theatre, Skate Park, and sample our delicious menu at the Snack Bar. We are open to the public, but specific attractions are available for private functions.
